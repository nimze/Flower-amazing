Romantic Flowers!!!

There are many types of flowers that mean love but the most iconic is the red rose.

Here are some flowers in order of romantic arrangement.

    Red rose

    The red rose reigns as the most romantic flower.

    Bright red rose represents romance, while burgundy symbolizes a love that is yet to be realized.

    The classic red rose is often the best choice for expressing your deepest affection.

    Tulip

    The meaning of tulip is perfect love

    Red tulips are most strongly associated with true love.

    Purple symbolizes royalty.

    Yellow tulips used to represent hopeless to now represent cheerful thoughts and sunshine.

    White tulips claim worthiness or send a message of forgiveness.

    Carnation

    The carnation symbolizesfascination and distinction.

    Lighter red shades of carnations are used to convey admiration.

    Darker shaded carnationsexpress deeper sentiments of love and affection.

    White carnations express purity and luck.

    Pink carnations is a sign of gratitude.
